Who's Talking?

Who's Talking (beta) helps you to track time spent in conversations and visualizing it through exporting the data.

I made this app out of curiosity and personal needs to figure out if a subjective tool and real-life data can better help us reflect on where time is spent (and not spent). And in some cases a showing a pie-chart speaks louder than words ;)

I'm Alexander Skepp and I'm an Agile Coach. Feedback on improvements and how you are using this tool is highly appreciated: webmaster@alphagoogle.com

Thanks,
Alex

Ps. Press and hold to rename a timer and press and drag to move timers around on the screen.
