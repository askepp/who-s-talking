Who's Talking?

Who's Talking (beta) helps you to track and visualize time spent in conversations.

I made this tool out of curiosity to find out if and how real-life data can help us reflect on how we spend our time together. And in some cases showing a pie-chart to someone who is either talking too much or too little speaks louder than words.

I'm Alexander Skepp and I'm an Agile Coach. Improvement suggestions and feedback on how you are using this tool is highly appreciated: webmaster@alphagoogle.com

Thanks,
Alex

Ps. Press and drag to move elements around on the screen to make it resemble your environment and press and hold a timer to update it.
